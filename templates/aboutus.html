About CoolWater

CoolWater is a licenced non-life insurer and financial services provider, offering customers a range of non-life car insurance products.

Our major shareholder is Santam, a blue-chip JSE-listed company, giving CoolWater enough financial muscle and credibility to help build our unique brand identity.

At CoolWater, we are fanatical about delivering an awesome customer experience. We view this as a real differentiator and are passionately committed to ensuring that we always keep to our promises, and deliver the type of service that our customers deserve.

Why CoolWater?

CoolWater enables you to:

  • Choose the type of insurance cover that suits your needs and your pocket, simple to understand, without the fine-print
  • Decide your preferred way to interact with us: via our call centre or online: 24/7/365
  • Experience a hassle-free and efficient claims process
  • Choose flexible excesses
